Colonoscope Repair

Expert repair and rebuild of colonoscopes to OEM standards for optimal performance.

Comprehensive Colonoscope Servicing

Colonoscopes undergo significant wear and tear. We provide full-service repair and maintenance to ensure optimal functioning and image clarity. Our technicians are trained to handle minor adjustments to complete rebuilds.

Common Repairs

  • Insertion Tube Replacement: Fixes kinks, bites, and deep scratches using OEM-grade materials with RIT (Responsive Insertion Technology) optimization.
  • Bending Section Re-build: Complete re-meshing and angulation wire tensioning to restore precise control and maneuverability.
  • Biopsy & Suction Channel Replacement: Installation of high-durability internal channels to resolve leaks and blockages.
  • Image Sensor (CCD) Repair: Restoration of high-definition imaging, color balance, and electronic connector stability.
  • Air/Water Channel Clearing: High-pressure flushing and nozzle replacement to ensure optimal lens cleaning during procedures.

Specialized Services

  • Leak Testing & Seal Repair: Comprehensive diagnostic to prevent fluid invasion and internal corrosion.
  • Light Guide Bundle Replacement: Restore optimal illumination for crystal-clear diagnostics.
  • Distal End Polishing: Removing micro-scratches from the objective lens for peak optical performance.
  • Video Connector Re-termination: Repairing damaged pins and logic boards within the one-touch connector.

Our Process

We conduct an exhaustive 40-point inspection before and after any repair to ensure the scope meets all OEM specifications and safety standards.
